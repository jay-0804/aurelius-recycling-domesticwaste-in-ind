# Product Requirements Document (PRD): Recycling Domestic Waste in India
## Vision
Our vision is to create a user-friendly, efficient, and sustainable recycling platform that empowers Indian households to contribute to a cleaner environment, while also providing a reliable source of raw materials for manufacturers. We aim to revolutionize the way India manages domestic waste, making recycling easy, accessible, and rewarding for all stakeholders.

## Problem
The current waste management system in India is inadequate, leading to significant environmental and health concerns. The lack of proper waste segregation, collection, and recycling infrastructure results in a substantial amount of waste ending up in landfills or being burned, contributing to pollution and climate change. Existing recycling solutions are often fragmented, inefficient, and expensive, making it difficult for households to participate in recycling programs.

## Solution
Our solution, "GreenCycle," is a mobile-based platform that connects households with local recycling centers, enabling easy and convenient recycling of domestic waste. The platform will provide:

* User-friendly interface for households to schedule waste pickups
* Real-time tracking of waste collection and recycling processes
* Educational resources and incentives to promote recycling practices
* Partnerships with local recycling centers to ensure efficient and responsible waste management
* Integration with existing waste management infrastructure to minimize costs and maximize impact

## Target Users
Our primary target users are:

* Urban Indian households with a medium to high disposable income
* Environmentally conscious individuals and families
* Local recycling centers and waste management agencies
* Manufacturers seeking reliable sources of recycled raw materials

## MVP Features
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include:

1. **User Registration**: Household registration and profile creation
2. **Waste Scheduling**: Scheduling of waste pickups and tracking of collection status
3. **Recycling Education**: Access to educational resources and tips on recycling best practices
4. **Partnership with Local Recycling Centers**: Integration with local recycling centers for efficient waste management
5. **Incentives and Rewards**: Implementation of a reward system to encourage recycling participation

## Metrics
To measure the success of GreenCycle, we will track the following key performance indicators (KPIs):

1. **User Acquisition**: Number of registered households
2. **Waste Collection Rate**: Percentage of scheduled waste pickups completed successfully
3. **Recycling Rate**: Percentage of collected waste that is recycled
4. **User Engagement**: Frequency of user interactions with the platform (e.g., scheduling waste pickups, accessing educational resources)
5. **Customer Satisfaction**: User feedback and ratings of the platform

## Roadmap
Our development roadmap is divided into three phases:

1. **Phase 1 (0-3 months)**: Research, planning, and development of the MVP
2. **Phase 2 (3-6 months)**: Launch of the MVP, user acquisition, and iteration based on user feedback
3. **Phase 3 (6-12 months)**: Expansion of features, partnerships with manufacturers, and scaling of the platform

## User Stories
The following user stories will guide our development process:

1. **As a household user, I want to easily schedule waste pickups so that I can contribute to a cleaner environment.**
2. **As a recycling center operator, I want to receive notifications of scheduled waste pickups so that I can plan my collection routes efficiently.**
3. **As a manufacturer, I want to access a reliable source of recycled raw materials so that I can reduce my production costs and environmental impact.**
4. **As a user, I want to access educational resources and tips on recycling best practices so that I can improve my recycling habits.**
5. **As a user, I want to receive incentives and rewards for participating in the recycling program so that I can stay motivated to continue recycling.**

By following this PRD, we aim to create a successful and sustainable recycling platform that makes a positive impact on the environment and contributes to the growth of the Indian recycling market.
